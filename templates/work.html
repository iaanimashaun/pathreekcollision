People sometimes ask me about interesting places to go work. I of course think that Stripe is a good answer. But here are some other options to consider:

  • Longevity.vc. Understanding the mechanisms that regulate aging could yield ways to treat many other diseases more effectively. Furthermore, Laura is a quite remarkable individual.
  • Our World in Data. To generate progress, we should understand how we're doing. Our World in Data is dedicated to quantitatively analyzing some of our most important global issues.
  • Pioneer. Pioneer is running a global talent radar. If it works, it could be a very big deal. They're hiring engineers.
  • Tyler Cowen has a list of interesting problems to work on.
